Best Movies According To Nicole

My top 3 movies of all time

My Blue Heaven

A quirky movie about a gangster who is relocated to a small town under withness protection program.

Grey Man

An action packed movie. Clandestine assassin is targeted by his handlers.

Anne of Green Gables

Set in the late 1800's an orphan is adopted by two aging siblings to help on the farm but becomes loved and adored despite her mischevious antics.
